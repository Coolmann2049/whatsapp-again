CSV Format Guidelines
Required Columns:
  • phone (Required, international format with country code)
  • name (Optional)
  • email (Optional)
Example Format:
phone,name,email,company
+1234567890,John Doe,john@example.com,Acme Inc
+9876543210,Jane Smith,jane@example.com,XYZ Corp
Important Notes:
  • First row must contain column headers.
  • Phone numbers must include the country code.
  • UTF-8 encoding is required.
